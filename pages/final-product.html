Target Audience

This toolkit is designed to be useful for:

  • Students: Learning about AI development with sustainability in mind
  • Researchers: Measuring and reporting the environmental impact of their experiments
  • Developers: Integrating green AI practices into production systems
  • Educators: Teaching sustainable computing principles
